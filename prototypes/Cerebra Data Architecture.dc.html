Stage 02 · Metric derivation
recall_accuracy = Σ wᵢ·(scoreᵢ/3) / Σ wᵢ
problem_accuracy = Σ wᵢ·correctᵢ / Σ wᵢ
retention = exp(−Δt / stability)
mastery = 0.45·recall + 0.25·problem + 0.30·retention

Recency weight wᵢ = exp(−λ·age); Δt = now − last_reviewed_at. A small capped bonus from explanation quality feeds mastery.
